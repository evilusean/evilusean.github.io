Trig Cheatsheet & Quiz

How to Use

Cheatsheet Mode: Check the identities you want to study. By default, essential engineering identities are selected.

Quiz Mode: The app will cycle through selected identities. First shows the name, then formula, then usage (if enabled).

Controls: Use Prev/Next buttons or arrow keys. Press Space to save for review. Adjust speed with the slider.

Mobile: Swipe left/right to navigate in quiz mode.
