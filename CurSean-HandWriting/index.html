About & How to Use

How to Use:

  1. Type your text in the text box (supports multiple lines)
  2. Click "Generate Handwriting" to see the animation
  3. Customize:
    • Choose between Satisfy or Pacifico fonts
    • Pick your text color
    • Adjust text size (24-80px)
  4. Download: Save as PNG or SVG image
  5. Share: Click "Share URL" to copy a link with your settings

About This App:

Note: This app is not "high fidelity" cursive handwriting.

True cursive handwriting is written in one continuous stroke per word, without lifting the pen. This creates flowing, connected letters that are the hallmark of traditional cursive writing.

This tool uses digital fonts that approximate cursive style, but letters are rendered individually rather than as continuous strokes. It's great for visualization and practice, but not a replacement for learning proper cursive technique.
